The New Balance Kids' Collection

Fun, fresh and functional—New Balance is proud to offer top-notch kids’ shoes, apparel and more that offer the resiliency parents love with the look and feel your kids seek. Our kids’ collection of clothes and shoes focuses on durability and ease of use to provide the quality you seek for your children’s longest days on the playground.

Our kids' sneaker collection includes first walkers, little kid and big kid sizes. From laced designs to hook and loop closures and slip-on fits, our technology-backed kids’ shoes offer a little something for every age, preference and play style.

Your child’s apparel needs are covered with performance gear that wicks sweat, keeps them warm, and outlasts even the longest days of play and, of course. The New Balance’s kids’ collection is all about your kids’ comfort, offering functional, fashionable clothing that looks as amazing as it feels—and lasts even longer.
